> Campbell ghopdiets_proportion.csv
grazzhopperXplant species matrix indicating proportion of diet a plant species occupies for a given species of grasshopper species. Matrix was provided by Ellen Welti and was used in her 2018 paper. Original citation for the data:  Campbell, J. B., Arnett, W. H., Lambley, J. D., Jantz, O. K., & Knutson, H. (1974). Grasshoppers (Acrididae) of the Flint Hills native tallgrass prairie in Kansas. Agricultural Experimental Station, Kansas State University, Manhattan, KS, 19, 72–145.


> Campbell ghopdiets.csv
grazzhopperXplant species matrix indicating counts of grasshoppers with the corresponding plant found in their gut contents for each grasshopper species. Matrix was provided by Ellen Welti and was used in her 2018 paper. Original citation for the data:  Campbell, J. B., Arnett, W. H., Lambley, J. D., Jantz, O. K., & Knutson, H. (1974). Grasshoppers (Acrididae) of the Flint Hills native tallgrass prairie in Kansas. Agricultural Experimental Station, Kansas State University, Manhattan, KS, 19, 72–145.
